& $a, b \in \Z$, $a \leq b$

Bernoulli
& $p \in [0,1]$
& $p^k (1-p)^{1-k}$,\; $k \in \{0, 1\}$
& $\begin{cases} 1 - p & k = 0 \\ 1 & k = 1 \end{cases}$
& $p$
& $p(1-p)$
\\

Binomial
& $n \in \N$, $p \in [0,1]$
& $\dbinom{n}{k} p^k (1-p)^{n-k}$,\; $k \in \{0, \ldots, n\}$
& $\displaystyle\sum_{i=0}^{k} \dbinom{n}{i} p^i (1-p)^{n-i}$
& $np$
& $np(1-p)$
\\

Geometric
& $p \in (0,1]$
& $(1-p)^{k-1} p$,\; $k \in \{1, 2, \ldots\}$
& $1 - (1-p)^{ k }$
& $\dfrac{1}{p}$
& $\dfrac{1-p}{p^2}$
\\
